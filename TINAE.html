This is Nothing and Everything (Thing Thing) traces and celebrates the mundanity and playfulness of the overlooked. the world is a playground, a lush environment that encourages curiosity and discovery. A slew of ephemera, wonder, and airiness, this is as serious or as useless as you'd like it to be. Though, if you look hard enough, you may find more overlap than expected. join me in the fun, and maybe we can be friends, maybe you'll fall in love, maybe you'll be hungry, or maybe you will feel absolutely nothing. but i insist, something will change.
