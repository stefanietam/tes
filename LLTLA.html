STEFANIE TAM

INFORMATION





















12.2018; 8"X11"

A documentation of the Fashion, Flower, and Toy Districts of DTLA--an attempt to capture the multiculturalism, vibrance, and exuberance of LA. Including photos of the space, people, and textures, an index archiving the businesses of each distinctive district, and essays on the history of these areas and their past and present influence to the ever-changing landscape of LA, this series is my love letter to you, Los Angeles.
