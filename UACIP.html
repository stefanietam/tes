08.2017; 4.25"x5.5"

An interactive photoshoot with live-printed, personalized zines for the attendees of the UCLA's School of the Arts and Architecture 2017- 2018 Celebration.

After taking photos with friends, family, or just oneself, people navigate an online response form to include their name as well as a response to one of four lighthearted prompts that provide fun insight on these peoples' personalities. Working with live photos and data, each zine, including the past six peoples' photos, names, and responses to the prompts, reveals the faces of and some words from the people within the UCLA Arts community.

Approximately 70 unique zines were produced for the two hour event.
Photo direction by Hannah Burnett.
