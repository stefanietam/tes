Event collateral for Printed Matter's Annual Los Angeles Art Book Fair in 2019. For UCLA Collective's booth.

UCLA Collective at the LA Art Book Fair: D|MA and IS is a collaboration between students in Design | Media Arts and Information Studies. Our approach draws from research and practice in archives, design, information architecture, and multidisciplinary media creation to reconcile the meaning of material objects in a digital world. In showcasing student work, we seek to connect the academic realm with arts publishing and visual culture.
