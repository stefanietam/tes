GOODS + SERVICES is a 3D product catalogue of the future! Featuring a special edition issue with items curated from Craigslist's "BEST OF" section and four issues from their "FOR SALE" section, consumers can access 3D models of posted items to view and purchase! Find weird Craigslist postings that range between bellybutton lint, top ramen chicken flavor packets, and a Calder- esque dildo mobile!

G+S archives the cost, description, location, and post date of 49 Craigslist items over the span of 5 issues, each with a relative insert. Each item has a 3D modeled representation that's accessible through the Augment app in combination with the print issues.
